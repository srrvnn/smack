Student remanded

NASIK, SEPT. 16. A local court yesterday remanded a college student to judicial custody till September 29, for ``glorifying Saudi billionaire- terrorist, Osama bin Laden, and defending attacks on the U.S.''.

Police today said Shaikh Majid Shaikh Nuruddin, a student of Malegaon's Sayajirao Gaikwad College, had allegedly pasted a picture of bin Laden on college notice board and wrote a few lines ``defending his (Laden's) action and attacks on the U.S.''.

Various students and teachers organisations have strongly condemned the act and a ``bandh'' was observed yesterday demanding rustication of the student.

The Principal of the college had lodged a complaint against the student, leading to his arrest.
