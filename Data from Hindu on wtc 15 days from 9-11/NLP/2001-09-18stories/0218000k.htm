VHP launches 'jalabhishek'

NEW DELHI, SEPT. 17. Amid chanting of `mantras', the Vishwa Hindu Parishad today launched its nation-wide `jalabhishek' programme in the run up to the construction of Ram temple in Ayodhya which is scheduled to begin after the Mahashivratri on March 12.

The programme, comprising Lord Shiva's worship by offering of holy waters, was performed at the Gauri Shankar temple in Chandni Chowk under the aegis of Sri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir Nirman Jap Yagna Samiti.
