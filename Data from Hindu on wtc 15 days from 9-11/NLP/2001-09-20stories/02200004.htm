J&K militants support strike call against U.S

By Shujaat Bukhari

SRINAGAR, SEPT. 19. Expressing complete solidarity with the Taliban, Afghanistan and Osama bin Laden, four militant groups and the Dukhtaran-e-Millat (DeM) have extended support to a strike call given by the Islamabad-based Pak.-Afghan Defence Council for September 21 to protest U.S. plans for action against Afghanistan.

The joint statement issued by the Jaish-e-Mohammad, the Harkat-ul-Mujahideen, the Al Badr and the Jamiat-ul- Mujahideen said, ``American repression and imperialism is a challenge for the entire Muslim world,'' and added that it was surprising on the part of world media to link Osama with the bombing in New York and Washington and pave wave for America's ``sinister plans to strike against Afghanistan''. This, despite the fact that here was no evidence to prove his involvement.

The outfits warned transporters particularly auto rickshaw drivers to suspend their work on Friday.
