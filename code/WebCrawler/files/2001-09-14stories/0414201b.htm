Naidu plea to help Indian victims' kin

By Our Special Correspondent

HYDERABAD, SEPT. 13. The Chief Minister, Mr. N. Chandrababu Naidu, has appealed to the Prime Minister, Mr. A. B. Vajpayee, to render assistance to families of Indians who are among the victims of carnage at the World Trade Center in New York.

In a letter to Mr. Vajpayee today, Mr. Naidu said reports reaching here suggested that there were a large number of Indians among the victims at the World Trade Center. ``I wish to appeal to you to take the necessary steps for rendering assistance and succour to the families of the hapless victims from our country.''
