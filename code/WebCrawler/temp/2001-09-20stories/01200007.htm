India criticises Musharraf remarks

By Our Special Correspondent

NEW DELHI, SEPT. 19. India has taken strong exception to the attempt by the Pakistan President, Gen. Pervez Musharraf, to turn his televised address today into a tirade against New Delhi.

``Instead of focusing on terrorism, which is responsible for the present situation, it is most regrettable that the President of Pakistan continues to give voice to an anti-India tirade,'' the spokesperson of the Ministry of External Affairs said. Gen. Musharraf maintained an ``uncalled-for, unifocal approach'' while describing the ``most-challenging hour'' in Pakistan's history, she added.

The issue is not India-Pakistan relations, but terrorism.
