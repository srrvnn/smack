$ 25 million reward

WASHINGTON, SEPT. 23. The U.S. is offering a $ 25 million reward for information leading to the apprehension of wanted terrorist Osama bin Laden, according to the Secretary of State, Mr. Colin Powell, who hopes the cash could yield results.

This comes on top of the $ 5 million offered by the Federal Bureau of Investigation in 1999 for information leading to the arrest of Osama following bomb attacks on U.S. embassies in Kenya and Tanzania.

- AFP
